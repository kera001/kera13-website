GABONESE CULTRE


Gabon , officially the Gabonese Republic (French: RÃ©publique gabonaise), is a country on the Atlantic coast of Central Africa, on the equator, bordered by Equatorial Guinea to the northwest, Cameroon to the north, the Republic of the Congo on the east and south, and the Gulf of Guinea to the west.


The culture of Gabon includes all the cultural practices of the Gabonese people, inherited from the traditional culture of the different ethnic groups that make it up and from the modern Western influence. The current population is estimated in 2022 at 2.34 million Gabonese (compared to 1 million in 1995 and 500,000 in 1965).
